Exporter

Export all your notes from Apple Notes to Markdown/HTML with attachments.

Features of Exporter

Exporter prioritizes your data privacy by being a local-only app. Your data never leaves your mac.

Supports exporting to markdown or HTML format. Preserves folder structure, and notes metadata like creation and modification dates.

Supports exporting jpeg, png, gif, tiff, bmp, pdf, ics, m4a, mov, & mp3 attachments.

Supports exporting selected notebooks using filter-view provided as an in-app purchase feature.

Migrate from Apple Notes to other note-taking apps
