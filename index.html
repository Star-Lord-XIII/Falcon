Falcon

Writing notes made simple!

Create, Update & Organize notes easily.

Write Notes in markdown, easy-to-write and easy-to-read format. Organise them by tags & notebooks. Search notes by tags, inside app & finder.

Preview notes with formatting & images

Falcon supports adding images to your markdown notes. Read notes in elegantly formatted previews.

Live preview notes as you write them.

With live preview you can watch markdown text render into elegant HTML pages instantly. Preview is enabled with code-highlighting & image scaling to display notes perfectly.

Distraction free Write and Read modes.

Write & Read your notes in uncluttered and clean interface, to maximize your focus on the task!

Paint the app in colors of your choice.

Falcon app comes with 2 inbuilt themes - Light & Dark. You can modify color of almost all the components of the app from view-dividers to editor's cursor, through hex color-codes by modifying the default theme files. Create & share themes you build!
