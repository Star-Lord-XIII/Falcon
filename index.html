Falcon

A simple, markdown based note-taking app for Mac!

Write Notes in Markdown

Write Notes in easy-to-write and easy-to-read format. See markdown notes elegantly rendered in HTML format.

Organize and search Notes Easily

Organize Notes in various Notebooks according to their subjects. Search Notes by their title, content and tags.

Format Note content easily

Put **Emphasis** on content in you notes by adding formatting to textual content, or add images and links to your notes to make them more meaningful.
